Three Cool Facts About Orangutans!

  1. Orangutans are incredibly dexterous and can even use their feet to eat.
  2. Young orangutans stay with their mothers for up to 8 years.
  3. Orangutans use large leaves as umbrellas during rainstorms.

Some things Orangutans like eating

  • Mangoes
  • Plant stems
  • Wild honey
  • Insects
