Three Cool Facts About Chimpanzees!

  1. Chimpanzees use branches to "fish" for insects.
  2. Chimpanzees use leaves as sponges and even as medicine.
  3. Chimpanzees laugh and hug one another just like humans!

Some things Chimpanzees like eating

  • Fresh leaves
  • Seeds
  • Nuts
  • Wild honey
  • Ripe fruits
